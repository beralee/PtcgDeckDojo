# Psychic Attribute VFX Notes

## One-sentence rule

Psychic should read as compressed mind pressure: a luminous violet-white core, a cool cyan edge, and thin geometric psionic rings or shard petals.

## Package shape

- `cast + travel + impact`
- One dominant subject per asset
- Minimal support layers only
- No shared runtime edits

## Negative space rules

- Do not lean into Ghost or Darkness aesthetics.
- Do not use lightning language.
- Do not turn the impact into fireworks.
- Keep transparency clean and the silhouette readable.
