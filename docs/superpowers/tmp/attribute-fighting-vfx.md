# Fighting Attribute VFX Notes

## One-sentence rule

Fighting attacks should read as a heavy body-blow impact: bone-white flash, terracotta-red pressure core, sandy dust fracture, and compact angular shards.

## Package shape

- `impact-only`
- One dominant impact bloom
- Support layers only for punch flash, fracture dust, and residue
- No shared runtime edits

## Negative space rules

- Do not lean into flame or lightning language.
- Do not turn the hit into a beam or projectile.
- Do not use symbolic rings as the main subject.
- Keep transparency clean and the silhouette compact.
